About Wellmax Engineering

Engineering-driven industrial heating solutions built for performance, reliability, and long service life.

Who We Are

Wellmax Engineering is an industrial manufacturing company based in Pune, Maharashtra, specializing in the design and manufacture of industrial furnaces, heat treatment plants, industrial ovens, and customized metal fabrication solutions.

With hands-on experience in heat treatment equipment and industrial process systems, we focus on delivering application-specific solutions rather than generic designs.

Our Engineering Approach

Our systems are designed with emphasis on temperature uniformity, process reliability, energy efficiency, and operational stability.

Each project is engineered based on customer process requirements, component characteristics, and production goals.

Manufacturing & Support

In-house fabrication and assembly ensure quality control at every stage. We also provide installation, commissioning, and after-sales support for long-term operational confidence.
